Our Story

Founded in 2024, Terravast Holdings Pty Ltd emerged from a simple observation: buying and selling land in Botswana was fragmented and lacked trust. We built a digital platform that brings transparency, verification, and nationwide reach to the land market. Today, we connect thousands of buyers and sellers across all districts.
